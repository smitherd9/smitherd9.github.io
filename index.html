What Are The Chances?

Gathering restaurant inspection data from New York City's Department of Health and Mental Hygiene, it calculates a "Chances Rating" from 1 to 10 of the likelihood of a customer getting food poisoning. Users can search by restaurant name, zipcode or cuisine type.
