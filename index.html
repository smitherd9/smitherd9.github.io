What Are The Chances?

Gathering restaurant inspection data from New York City's Department of Health and Mental Hygiene, it calculates a "Chances Rating" from 1 to 10 of the likelihood of a customer getting food poisoning. Users can search by restaurant name, zipcode or cuisine type.

What Are The Chances?
-- Death in NYC

Searching government statistics on leading causes of death in New York City, this app then returns the most common cause of death adjusted for age based on the user's query. Search is enabled by ethnicity, sex or year. It also generates a C3-js bar chart showing other leading causes of death and their age adjusted death rates for the available years.

Old News

Intended for people with an interest in history and to counteract the negative effects of living in a world with a 24 hour news cycle, users can query the New York Times or the Guardian for articles in the recent past by date, by a date range or by a topic and date.
